About Us

Greenville Outdoor Living was founded by Blake Albrecht and Steven Bunn, South Carolina natives and Clemson graduates in Turfgrass Management. Their experience is not limited to one field — Blake has a background in construction and carpentry, and Steven has experience with botanical garden and garden design.

We started Greenville Outdoor Living in 2018 after doing numerous projects together, and realizing we shared similar dreams about the landscape industry and outdoor spaces. Our vision is to encourage sustainable landscapes, embrace horticulture, and shepherd the outdoor endeavors of the community in and around Greenville. We love our clients, and partnering with them to see their dreams in greenspaces come to life!
