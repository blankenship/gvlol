About Us

Greenville Outdoor Living creates modern, sustainable landscapes in the Greater Greenville area. Founded by Blake Albrecht and Steven Bunn, who met studying Turf Grass Management at Clemson University, our experience is not limited to one just field — Blake has a background in construction and carpentry, and Steven has experience with botanical gardening and garden design.

After college, we each started our own landscape companies, but quickly realized the need for creative landscapers, and joined forces to better serve our community. Greenville Outdoor Living offers a range of services, including Landscape Design, Installation, Hardscapes, Drainage, Irrigation and Maintenance, and more. We love our clients, and partnering with them to see their dreams in greenspaces come to life!
